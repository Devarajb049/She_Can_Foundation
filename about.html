Our Core Vision

Empowering Women, Fostering Equal Futures

The She Can Foundation is registered under the Indian Societies Registration Act of 1860, committed to resolving period poverty, improving healthcare accessibility, and bridging education gaps for marginalized girls and women.

We believe that real change begins by providing girls and women with the right tools. When we support one woman, we elevate an entire household and build a healthier community.

Skill Training

Vocational training courses to foster financial independence.

Period Dignity

Free pads, kits, and menstrual hygiene campaigns.

Education & Support Programs

Explore the specialized initiatives we carry out round the year to impact lives.

Education & Awareness

We run evening education centers and scholarship schemes for young girls at risk of school dropout. Additionally, we conduct menstrual hygiene workshops in rural schools to raise awareness and support healthy, safe, and positive experiences.

Community Support Initiatives

By offering health consultations, distributed dignity packages (hygiene products, guidance literature), and vocational workshops, we equip women to become financially independent while boosting their health and physical well-being.

Our Journey of Impact

A look at the major milestones that have defined our growth and presence.

2021

Foundation Established

Registered under the Societies Registration Act, shepherding our first chapter with just 10 passionate volunteers in Delhi NCR.

2022

1,000+ Girls Educated

Initiated digital literacy schemes, setting up local computer learning labs and enabling girls to learn foundational tech skills.

2024

Dignity Kits Launch

Kicked off our flagship Menstrual Hygiene program, distributing clean sanitary pads and awareness kits to schoolgirls.

2026

Expanding Horizons

Recognized for outstanding impact, scaling operations across multiple states with over 10,000 girls supported successfully.
